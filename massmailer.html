Mass Mailer
Mass Mailer is an email delivery program that allows users to contact multiple list of clients with just a click of a button.
Project Link GitHub

Introduction

When it comes to text communication, email is one of the only common denominators. Email marketing is vital for the success of many businesses. Mass Mailing allows the user to send their message to many clients at once.

One of the biggest issues in mass mailing is maintaining email server reputation. Even if you have a double opt-in list, there is no guarantee that your email server will continue maintain standings throughout its lifetime. There are some solutions out there but they tend to be expensive and they restrict the content of your email. The beauty behind Mass Mailer lies in its ability to send each email from a different SMTP server allowing you to bypass restrictions set by email service providers and email reputation algorithms. By adding multiple email servers you can rotate them per email recipient, lessening the reputation hit an email server would would get if it sends an email deemed to be seen as spam. You can also take advantage of the plethora of email service providers, such as Gmail and Yahoo.

Features
  • Manage clients email address
  • Import and export client information from a spreadsheet
  • Create and edit the email message to be sent in a drag-and-drop interface
  • Use multiple SMTP servers in a mailing list for better delivery rate
  • Modify and send from anywhere with an internet connection
