About

( Developer, Artist, Student, Dreamer )

While most go a lifetime searching for their passion, I am lucky enough to have found it. Since the age of eight I have been expressing myself through code. Equipped with an old v-tech pre-computer I started to create what my mind imagined. While most children were playing video games I was creating them. Since the day I wrote my first line of code, I have been developing on a daily basis. I dream of of a world driven by socially responsible software and I want to be part of it.

Apart from being a developer, I am also a student of life. My guitar and bass are other mediums I use to express my creativity. Running is another passion I hold dearly and is part of my daily ritual. Self improvement is key to the way I live. I believe we are just a collection of our habits and by improving them we can improve ourselves.
